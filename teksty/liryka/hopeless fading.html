hopeless/fading

so ill live forever in shame ill let you lose all of your blame and yet i still cant get away my hearts just too used to the pain

doubting if my tarnished soul will open once more will it ever be truly worth it to love passion hidden in a shroud of selfish desires desperates clinging on to their dying fires

i waste even more time gaslighting myself for being this lonely i wasnt prepared i dont long for your pity or another chance but for the memories of our fading romance

one day we'll be free from the shame we'll see that there is no one to blame that even if we're better off far away we still loved every minute no matter the pain
